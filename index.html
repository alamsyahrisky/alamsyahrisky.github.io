Research Development Apps

Research and Development (R&D) application is software that supports the R&D division in carrying out research to create new products. Starting from researching ingredients, flavors, product forms, to packaging, by utilizing this application, companies can improve their products. This application helps R&D produce a more efficient production process and reduce production costs.

To support the maximum use of the application, there are several supporting features for carrying out product research, as follows:

  • Competitor Product Module, which is used as material for comparison
  • Efficiency module, which covers product, packaging, and process efficiency
  • Trial module, which includes product trials, raw materials, and packaging
  • Sensorik test module
